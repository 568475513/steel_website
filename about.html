ABOUT COMPANY

ONE STEEL stands as a preeminent product service provider and exporter of steel pipes in China.

With an annual turnover reaching 100 million dollars, a workforce of over 100 dedicated employees, and two state-of-the-art factories specializing in Seamless (SMLS) and Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded (LSAW) steel pipes, we have significantly expanded our production capabilities. We've also invested in production lines for Electric Resistance Welded (ERW), Spiral Submerged Arc Welded (SSAW) steel pipes, as well as stainless - steel products.True to the essence of our name, "ONE", each letter embodies a core value:

"O" represents "Outstanding". ONE STEEL has firmly established itself as an outstanding player in the steel industry. Our continuous pursuit of innovation, advanced production techniques, and strict quality control measures have set us apart from the competition.

"N" stands for "Nobility". We uphold a noble working style in all aspects of our operations. We strive to be an unwaveringly trustworthy partner to our clients, maintaining the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and responsibility. This commitment is reflected not only in our business dealings but also in the exceptional quality we deliver across all product lines.

"E" denotes "Excellent". Our strength lies in our outstanding team members, who bring a wealth of expertise and passion to their work. Coupled with our top - notch customer services and unrivaled product quality, we are dedicated to exceeding customer expectations at every turn.

Guided by our core principle of "OUTSTANDING PRODUCT QUALITY, NOBILITY WORKING STYLE, EXCELLENT TEAMWORK", we are confident in our ability to offer customers high - quality products at highly competitive prices.
